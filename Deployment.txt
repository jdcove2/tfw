 1. Show pom.xml file.
 2. Ensure on "master" branch by looking at left column.
 3. Press pencil icon on right to edit the pom.xml file.
 4. Remove "-SNAPSHOT" from version field.
 5. Press "Commit Changes" button in upper right.
 6. Change commit message to "Release <version>" (<version> from pom.xml).
 7. Make sure "Commit directly to master branch" is selected.
 8. Press "Commit Changes" button.
 9. Go back to main project screen.
10. Press the "Releases" label on the right side.
11. Press "Draft a new release" button in upper-right.
12. Press "Choose a tag".
12. Enter the new <version>.
13. Press "Create new tag: <version>".
14. Ensure target is "master".
15. Enter <version> in Release Title field.
16. Ensure "Set as a pre-release" is unchecked.
17. Ensure "Set as the latest release" is checked.
18. Press "Publish release" button.
19. Check "Actions" to make sure everything went ok.
20. Go back to main project screen.
21. Show pom.xml file.
22. Ensure on "master" branch by looking at left column.
23. Press pencil icon on right to edit the pom.xml file.
24. Increment <version> and add "-SNAPSHOT" to version field.
25. Press "Commit Changes" button in upper right.
26. Change commit message to "Prepare for next development iteration".
27. Make sure "Commit directly to master branch" is selected.
28. Press "Commit Changes" button.
